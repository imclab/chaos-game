D3 - Neo4j

A demos app using Neo4j to display recipe data using d3.js

Ingredients

(Small light green nodes)

Doubleclick - Filters data to a selection of recipes that contain that ingredient (and the ingredients of those recipes). If there are more than 10 that match, a random selection of 10 recipes is returned.

Recipes

(Larger dark green nodes)

Click - Click and drag to fix the recipe nodes in position.

Doubleclick - Doubleclick to release a fixed recipe node.

Selected

(Dark red node)

The dark red node is the current ingredient node that the graph is centered around. That ingredient appears in all the recipes in the current graph.
